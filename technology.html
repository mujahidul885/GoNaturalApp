Our Technology

Harnessing the power of mycelium to create sustainable packaging solutions

MycoComposite™

Our proprietary technology combines mycelium (mushroom roots) with agricultural byproducts to create a strong, lightweight material that can be grown into any shape.

Growth Process

We grow materials in just 7 days using a carbon-negative process that requires minimal energy compared to traditional manufacturing.
