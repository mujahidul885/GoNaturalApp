Product Description

Our custom Mushroom® Packaging is grown to fit your specific product dimensions, providing superior protection while being 100% home compostable. Each piece is grown to order using our MycoComposite™ technology.

Key Benefits

  • Custom Fit: Grown to match your product's exact dimensions for perfect protection
  • Sustainable: Made from mycelium and agricultural byproducts
  • Performance: Comparable protection to plastic foams with better vibration dampening
  • Circular: At end of life, simply break up and compost at home

How It's Made

We combine hemp hurd (agricultural waste) with mycelium in custom molds. Over 7 days, the mycelium grows through the hurd, binding it into a solid form. The material is then dehydrated to stop the growth process, resulting in a lightweight, protective packaging solution.

Technical Specifications

Material Composition Mycelium (100% pure, no additives) and hemp hurd
Density 8-15 lb/ft³ (adjustable based on requirements)
Compression Strength 10-50 psi (depending on density)
Water Resistance Naturally hydrophobic, can withstand incidental moisture
Flame Resistance Self-extinguishing, meets ASTM E84 Class A
Temperature Resistance Stable from -40°F to 200°F
Shelf Life 30+ years in dry conditions

Composting Your Mushroom Packaging

One of the greatest benefits of Mushroom® Packaging is its compostability. Here's how to return it to the earth:

1. Break It Up

Break the packaging into smaller pieces (1-2 inches) to speed decomposition.

2. Add to Compost

Mix pieces into your compost pile or bin with other organic matter.

3. Wait

Typically decomposes in 30-45 days in active compost.

Note: While industrial composting works, home composting is preferred as it completes the sustainable cycle without transportation emissions.
